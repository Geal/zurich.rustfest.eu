--- layout: session permalink: /sessions/:name type: talk speakers: - pierre title: "Mistakes to avoid when writing a wrapper around a C library" desc: | Do you need to perform a certain task in Rust? Why not pick a mature C library that already does what you want, and write a wrapping library around it? Writing a wrapping library is sometimes not as easy as one could think, and you will probably make mistakes that will make you lose time or even make your wrapper unsound.
